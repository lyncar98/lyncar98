# Lyndon Carlson

**I lead AI strategy and build the systems behind it.** Director of the AI Practice at [Trissential](https://www.trissential.com), founding CTO of ChatLPO, and founder of [Lautum.AI](#) — turning the governance control plane that every serious AI platform grows into a product.

I work at the seam between executive AI strategy and production engineering: defining how enterprises move from Copilot pilots to governed, evaluated, custom agent systems — and writing the code that proves the pattern works.

### What I do

- **Set AI strategy for the enterprise** — consulting offerings, secure reference architectures, governance patterns, and delivery models that take organizations from experimentation to measurable, governed production.
- **Build evaluation as a release contract** — grader registries, `pass@1` / `pass@k` / `pass^k` consistency scoring, calibrated LLM judges, and eval gates that produce a real pass/fail instead of a dashboard number.
- **Design AI governance you can query** — an org → project → agent standards cascade, immutable release bundles, and a single control library mapped to NIST, ISO/IEC 42001, and the EU AI Act.
- **Lead teams and communities** — built Trissential's AI practice and an internal community of practice; founded and scaled ChatLPO from concept to production with a team of 10+ engineers; mentor founders through Startup Twin Cities.
